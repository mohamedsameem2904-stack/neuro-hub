Vascular ICD-10: I63

Ischemic Stroke (CVA)

Acute localized occlusion of a cerebral artery leading to a severe restriction of blood flow, resulting in rapid ischemic cascade damage and focal neurological deficits.

Clinical Presentation:

Contralateral hemiparesis, sudden facial drooping, acute dysarthria, and visual field disruptions.

Neurodegenerative ICD-10: G30

Alzheimer's Disease

Progressive, irreversible neurodegenerative disorder characterized histopathologically by extracellular beta-amyloid plaques and intracellular neurofibrillary tau tangles.

Clinical Presentation:

Short-term episodic memory deficits, geographic disorientation, executive dysfunction, and progressive language decline.

Paroxysmal ICD-10: G40

Epileptic Seizure Disorders

Chronic neurological disorder categorized by recurrent, unprovoked seizures originating from hypersynchronous, abnormal electrical neuronal discharges in the brain layer.

Clinical Presentation:

Altered state of consciousness, tonic muscle stiffening followed by rhythmic clonic jerking, or brief staring spells.

Demyelinating ICD-10: G35

Multiple Sclerosis (MS)

Immune-mediated inflammatory disease targeting central nervous system myelin, generating disseminated plaques throughout the white matter pathways.

Clinical Presentation:

Optic neuritis, Lhermitte's sign, sensory loss, motor weakness, and cerebellar ataxia episodic patterns.
